Sherubtse College Job Vacancy

Position: Site Supervising Engineer

Position Level 7
Qualification Minimum of Bachelor's in Civil Engineering with two years of experience in relevant field
Mode of Employment Contract
Number of Slots 1
Application Deadline 18th November, 2024, 4.00 PM

How to Apply

Eligible candidates may submit an application via the online portal RUB IMS. Applicants are required to register and apply through this platform.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a Bhutanese citizen.
  • Must be at least 18 years of age and not more than 45 years.
  • Must meet the specified qualifications and experience requirements.

Required Documents

  • Degree Certificate of Bachelor’s degree or provisional certificate.
  • Mark sheets for Bachelor’s degree, Class XII, and Class X.
  • Valid Security Clearance Certificate.
  • Valid Medical Fitness Certificate.
  • Valid Citizenship ID card.
  • Valid Audit Clearance Certificate (for employed applicants).
  • No Objection Certificate for employed candidates (if selected).
  • Experience Certificate(s) or documentary evidence of employment records.

Selection Process

Shortlisted candidates will be announced on the college website and contacted for an interview.

Notes

  • Two professional referees are required. If only one employment record, use one professional referee and one academic referee.
  • Incomplete applications will be rejected.
  • Applicants must ensure all mandatory documents are submitted online.
  • A Negative Drug Certificate is required if selected.
