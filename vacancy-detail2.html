Sherubtse College Job Vacancy

Sherubtse College is pleased to announce the following vacancies for various administrative positions for immediate recruitment.

Available Positions

Position Title Position Level Eligibility Criteria Slot Mode of Employment
Assistant ICT Officer/ICT Officer 8/7 Candidates must have a minimum aggregate of 60% each in Class X (English + best 4 subjects) and Class XII (English + best 3 subjects), and 55% in Bachelor's degree (honors)/60% in Bachelor's degree (general).
(Criteria do not apply for those with minimum of five years relevant experience)		 1 Regular
ICT Technical Associate 10 Candidates must have a minimum aggregate of 60% in Diploma and 50% each in Class X (English + best 4 subjects) and Class XII (English + best 3 subjects). 1 Regular

Application Deadline: 4th November, 2024, 4.00 PM

How to Apply

Eligible candidates may submit an application via the online portal RUB IMS. Applicants are required to register and apply through this platform.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a Bhutanese citizen.
  • Must be at least 18 years of age and not more than 45 years.
  • Must meet the specified qualifications and experience requirements.

Required Documents

  • Diploma/Bachelor’s Degree Certificate (or provisional if awaiting award).
  • Mark sheets for Diploma/Bachelor's degree, Class XII, and Class X.
  • Valid Security Clearance Certificate.
  • Valid Medical Fitness Certificate.
  • Valid Citizenship ID card (both sides).
  • Valid Audit Clearance Certificate (for employed applicants).
  • No Objection Certificate for employed candidates (if selected).
  • Experience Certificate(s) or documentary evidence of employment records.

Additional Notes

  • Two professional references are required, from different organizations if employed. If there’s only one employment record, use one professional referee and one academic referee.
  • Incomplete applications will be rejected.
  • Applicants must ensure all mandatory documents are submitted online.
  • Negative Drug Certificate is required if selected.
